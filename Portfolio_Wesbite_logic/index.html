    About Me

    My professional Career started at CiCi's Pizza where I worked as a Cook. Quickly becoming an Assistant Manager after 2 months employed. During my time as a cook and manager, I learned quickly the importance of teamwork under stressful timelines and the ability to manage multiple people to achieve a common goal. The greatest professional development I acquired from this position was, how to speak to clients in a way to answer all questions and eliminate any risk of miscommunications. From which assisted me in starting and excelling in The United States Army. I joined the Army in November of 2016 and served until August of 2021. My military experience sets me apart from other employees, as during this time I learned countless leadership qualities, skills, attributes and the ability to express compassion within a professional threshold. I attained the rank of Sergeant and title of 'FSC Maintenance Control Squad Leader'. During my last year of Service I managed a squad the size of 9 Soldiers, to ensure Mission Readiness of more then 150 vehicles, weapons, and safety equipment worth more then $200 million. My greatest achievement in this time was during a field exercise, when more then 85% of my company were unavailable due to a internal COVID-19 outbreak leaving myself in charge along with 5 other Soldiers from different Squads. I managed to successfully manage my team and maintain readiness of 200+ Battalion Vehicles, and 600+ Battalion Weapons during a 14 day training period.
